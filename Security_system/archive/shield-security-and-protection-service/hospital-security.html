Hospital Security

Although it is similar to the security of the plaza and business center in its main details, there are two critical points in this service. The first of these is emergency room security. While there is a very fine line between life and death, the most important point of this service is to be prepared, to predict and prevent the reactions of the relatives of the patients who apply or are brought here. Another important issue in this service is the safety of the newborn unit. Child/infant abduction risk is the biggest risk assessment of this service. Against this risk, it is most important that the staff cooperate with the hospital staff and be trained by experts.
