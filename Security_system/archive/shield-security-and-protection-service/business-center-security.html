Business Center Security

First of all, risk analysis of the Business Center / Plaza, which we provide security for, is performed, and risky areas are determined and an electronic security system is established. Building entrances and exits are secured and the inside of the building is monitored 24/7 with cameras. In this way, the damages that may occur to the life and property safety of the fixtures in the building and the employees are determined in advance and necessary measures are taken. By getting information about who the incoming guests are coming from, the person they come to is called and confirmation is requested. If approval is given, a visitor card is issued and a record is kept. Necessary guidance is made to the destination by making a body search. The identity of the visitor is returned by taking the signature of the visitor when he exits the building.
